The Department Plans Buys a New Ladder Truck

By: Joshua Clark, 8-10-2015

Due to aging apparatus, the department has approved the purchase of a new fire response vehicle. The department is replacing its aging ladder truck with a newer model. This will ensure the continued operation of the department’s fire protection activities and reduce maintenance costs in the long run.

The department is currently evaluating potential replacement vehicles from several manufacturers. The following table lists the vehicles currently being evaluated.

Manufacturers Model Length Cost
Spartan ERV Rearmount 103 ft ~$150,000
Pierce Mfg Ascendant 107 ft ~$200,000
E-One HPS 105 105 ft ~$175,000
E-One Teleboom 50 ft ~$95,000
